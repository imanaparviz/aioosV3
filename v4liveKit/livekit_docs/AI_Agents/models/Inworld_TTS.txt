Voices

LiveKit Inference supports all of the default voices available in the Inworld API. You can explore the available voices in the

Inworld TTS Playground

(free account required), and use the voice by copying its name into your LiveKit agent session.

Cloned voices unavailable

Cloned voices are not yet supported in LiveKit Inference. To use these voices, create your own Inworld account and use the

Inworld plugin

for LiveKit Agents instead.
